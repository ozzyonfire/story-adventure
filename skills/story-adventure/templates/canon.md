# Canon

## Immutable Facts
- Facts that must remain true in every branch.

## World Constraints
- Magic, technology, social, or physical limits.

## Character Truths
- Protagonist values, fears, and non-negotiable traits.
- Key NPC motivations and leverage.

## Continuity Guardrails
- Timeline anchors that cannot be contradicted.
- Secret information rules and reveal conditions.

## Prohibited Contradictions
- Explicitly list outcomes that are not allowed.
